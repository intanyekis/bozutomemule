Overall Meaning

The lyrics to The Pretty Things' song Mony Mony are an upbeat and energetic expression of the singer's intense attraction to a woman named Mony. The opening line, "here she comes now, sayin' come on, Mony", sets the scene for the rest of the song, as the singer is invited by Mony to join her in a wild and frenzied adventure. The lines "shoot 'em down, turn around, come home, Mony" suggest that Mony is a bold and adventurous person who has a headstrong personality and is not afraid to take risks. The repeated refrain of "Mony Mony" throughout the song serves to reinforce this idea of Mony as a dynamic and exciting force in the singer's life.


The second half of the song continues in the same vein, with the singer expressing his love for Mony and asking her to continue to show him affection. The lines "you got me tossin' and turnin' in the middle of the night, now" and "don't stop your cookin', it makes me feel so good, now" convey a sense of intense passion and desire. The singer seems to be completely enamored with Mony and willing to do whatever it takes to keep her love.


Overall, the lyrics to Mony Mony are an unabashed celebration of youthful energy, desire, and passion. The singer is clearly caught up in the thrill of the moment and is willing to throw caution to the wind in pursuit of his love for Mony.
