Eric Winter Height: How tall is Eric Winter?

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Friday, July 26, 2024

Days of Our Lives actor Eric Winter stands at 6 ft 1 inch (1.85 m & 185 cm) tall.

Winter also weighs 80 kg.

Who Is Eric Winter?

Eric Winter is an American actor cum model famous for starring as Rex Brady on the long-running soap opera “Days of Our Lives”, and as Dash Gardiner in the supernatural drama series “Witches of East End”.

Born on July 17, 1976, in La Mirada, California, Winter began his career in the entertainment industry as a model, appearing in campaigns for luxury brands such as Tommy Hilfiger, Ralph Lauren, and Calvin Klein.

He made his acting debut in the early 2000s, and has since appeared in numerous TV shows and films.

Some of the films in which Winter starred include “Days of Our Lives”, “Witches of East End”, “The Rookie”, “The Ugly Truth”, “Harold & Kumar Escape from Guantanamo Bay”, “Fire with Fire”, and the fantasy TV series “Fantasy Island”.

Winter also had recurring roles in series such as “Brothers & Sisters” and “The Mentalist.”

In addition to acting, Winter has hosted the reality series “Breakthrough with Tony Robbins,” where he worked together with popular life coach Tony Robbins.
