Keira Knightley is one of the most underrated actresses in Hollywood. With a career from early childhood, her acting prowess in blockbuster movies and TV series is never lacking. According to reports, Knightley's earnings from a single film range between $5 million to $7 million. Besides her acting career, she is also the brand ambassador for Chanel, which, according to Campaign UK, the actress has an annual contract worth $1 million.

Knightley was included in the 2007 Forbes list of the richest UK stars under 30, but she lives a frugal lifestyle with her husband, James Righton. Knightley explained that living a lavish lifestyle can cause alienation from people who do not share similar means. The actress believes that her best experiences have come from less luxurious settings and that she does not want to be defined by material possessions.

8 How Much Was Keira Knightley's Paid For The Pirates Of The Caribbean Movie Franchise?

Pirates of the Caribbean: The Curse of the Black Pearl is a highly successful movie that was released in 2003, as the first film in the Pirates of the Caribbean franchise. Keira Knightley played the role of Princess Elizabeth Swann in the movie alongside other notable actors such as Johnny Depp and Orlando Bloom. The film achieved tremendous success at the box office, grossing $654.3 million worldwide, making it the fourth highest-grossing film of 2003. The movie was also recognized for its groundbreaking special effects and production design.

The sequel, Pirates of the Caribbean: Dead Man's Chest, is the highest-grossing film in the franchise, earning over a billion dollars worldwide and becoming the highest-grossing movie of Knightley's career. The third installment of the franchise, At World's End, was released in 2007, with Knightley reprising her role. Like the previous films, the movie was a commercial success, grossing a massive $960.9 million worldwide and becoming the highest-grossing film of 2007.

According to reports, Knightley earned $5 million during the second installment of the Pirates of the Caribbean. It was also reported that her salary increased during the movie's third installment, but the exact figure has not been disclosed.

7 How Much Was Keira Knightley's Salary For the Atonement?

Atonement is a 2007 romantic war drama movie, that is considered one of the greatest love stories of all time. James McAvoy and Keira Knightley starred in the lead roles, with the plot based on a novel of the same name.

Knightley played Cecilia Tallis, the love interest of McAvoy's character, Robbie Turner. The movie received widespread critical acclaim and numerous awards, including an Academy Award and a BAFTA award. It also earned $131 million at the worldwide box office while Knightley reportedly earned $4 million for her role in the film.

6 How Much Was Keira Knightley's Salary For Her Role In Anna Karenina?

Keira Knightley's portrayal of Anna Karenina in the eponymous film adaptation has been widely praised by critics for her expressive and charismatic performance. Knightley's interpretation of the character is sympathetic yet complex, and she effortlessly captures the authenticity and passion of Anna Karenina's actions.

Her performance was the highlight of the film, and she was nominated for several awards, including the Academy Award for Best Actress in a Leading Role. The film won an Oscar for Best Costume Design, and Knightley reportedly earned $7 million for her critically acclaimed performance.

5 How Much Was Keira Knightley's Salary For The Imitation Game?

The Imitation Game is a biographical drama film based on the life of Alan Turing, a renowned cryptanalyst and considered the father of computer science. The movie depicts Turing's race against time with his team at Britain's top-secret Government Code and Cypher School during World War II. Keira Knightley's character is a brilliant young mathematician, Joan Clarke, whom Turing married to help her avoid conservative parents.

Although the film was condemned for its historical inaccuracies and criticized for the portrayal of Turing's homosexuality, Knightley was paid $5.5 million for her role in the film.

4 How Much Was Keira Knightley's Salary For the Film Everest?

The movie Everest depicts the real-life events of a fatal storm on the mountain in 1996, which claimed the lives of eight people attempting to descend the highest mountain in the world.

Keira Knightley portrayed the character of Jan Arnold, the pregnant wife of mountaineer and mountain guide Rob Hall in the action-adventure film. Although she is not directly involved in the expedition, Knightley’s character is tied to the events of the film through phone calls with her husband.

The movie faced production delays including an avalanche that threatened the crew filming in Nepal. This prompted most of Knightley's scenes to be filmed inside a studio in London. Despite this, she reportedly received a salary of around $6-8 million for her work on the film.

3 How Much Did Keira Knightley Earn For Her Role In Domino?

Keira Knightley played the character of Domino Harvey in the 2005 action-crime film Domino, which is based on Harvey’s real-life story. The plot revolves around the life of Domino Harvey, the daughter of actor Laurence Harvey and fashion model Paulene Stone. Harvey was a model but later became a bounty hunter, accused of robbing an armored car.

2 How Much Was Keira Knightley Paid For Collateral Beauty?

Collateral Beauty is a 2016 film about a New York advertising executive (played by Will Smith), withdrawing from life after a tragedy. Despite the efforts of his friends, he writes letters to Love, Time, and Death seeking answers from the universe.
