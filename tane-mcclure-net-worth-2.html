Tane McClure Wiki: Salary, Married, Wedding, Spouse, Family

Tane McClure was born on June 8, 1958 in Pacific Palisades, California, USA. She is an actress and editor, known for Legally Blonde (2001), Fear and Loathing in Las Vegas (1998) and Go (1999). She has been married to Gary Arendtz since July 17, 2001. They have one child. She was previously married to Jonathan Cain. ﻿﻿﻿﻿﻿
