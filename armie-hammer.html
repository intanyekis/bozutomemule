Armie Hammer News - Us Weekly

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Wednesday, August 21, 2024

Name:Armie Hammer

Hometown:Santa Monica, CA

Relationship:Single

Birthday:August 28, 1986

About

Armie Hammer made his feature film debut in 2006’s Flicka before gaining wider recognition for portraying twins Cameron and Tyler Winklevoss in The Social Network (2010). He went on to appear in J. Edgar (2011), The Lone Ranger (2013), Call Me by Your Name (2017) — for which he earned a Golden Globe nomination for Best Supporting Actor — and On the Basis of Sex (2018).

In 2021, Hammer was accused of sending text messages to various women containing sexual fantasies of rape, mutilation and cannibalism. Hammer was further accused of raping a woman in 2017, but he denied all allegations. The Los Angeles Police Department declined to file charges against the actor, citing insufficient evidence after their 2023 investigation.

Hammer previously married Elizabeth Chambers in 2010, splitting in 2020. The exes reached a divorce settlement in 2023 and share children Harper and Ford.
