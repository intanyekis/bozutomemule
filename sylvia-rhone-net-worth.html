Check Out Sylvia Rhone' Net Worth!

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Wednesday, July 3, 2024

While the world enjoys the music of top musicians like Travis Scott, Camila Cabello, and DJ Khalid, the person behind the scenes hardly gets any recognition. Sylvia Rhona, an American music industry executive and the face of Epic Records, is responsible for glamorizing top music and musicians.

The 67-year-old is the Chief Executive Officer of Epic Records, a significant division of the global Sony Entertainment. The diva remains an inspirational figure for every woman in the world, being the first woman to become the CEO of a major label by a Fortune 500 Company.

How Much is Sylvia Rhone’s Net Worth?

Hold your breath, cause Sylvia Rhone’s fortune is quite huge. The lady mastermind owns a staggering net worth of $20 million as of 2020. The diva worked through numerous corporate and music records for more than four decades to accumulate such a big number.

The primary source of her colossal net worth is from her hard work and a sharp business mind. Today, she is the CEO of Epic Records, who oversaw some of the top singles like Astroworld and Havana. Before that, she served as the president of Motown Records and CEO of Elektra Entertainment Group.

Worked with the likes of ACDC, Metallica, and Tracy Chapman

Who wouldn’t love a job with the likes of top international bands like ACDC and Metallica? Well, Sylvia Rhone lived the dream of billions as she helped the groups to commercialize their top pieces of music. Moreover, she worked alongside Tracy Chapman, Jason Mraz, and Busta Rhymes.

In the Sony entertainment, she oversees Camila Cabello, Meghan Trainor, and DJ Khalid. Moreover, she worked with Akon, Lil Wayne, and Chamillionaire in Motown Records.

Sylvia Rhone: Atlantic Records to Sony Music

The underrated artist, Sylvia Rhone, has a widely decorated portfolio with experiences in Atlantic Records, Warner Music Group, and Motown Records. Today, she serves as the CEO of the home to top artists, Epic Records.

With over four decades into the industry, she joined Sony entertainment’s Epic Records in 2012 and looked pleased there. In an interview, she expressed how thrilled she is with her tenure at the record company with the set of incredible artists.

Sylvia Rhone’s Awards and Recognitions 

The fantastic work of glamorizing the famous top singers of the country led Sylvia Rhone to many awards. In 2019, she received the rank of 35th on Billboard’s Annual Power 100 List.

Earlier, she also bagged the award of the Harlem Business Alliance Award in 2017. Likewise, she won the Black Girls Rock! Corporate Award in 2007 and Woman of The Year from Ms.Magazine in 2001 alongside Serena and Venus Williams.

Quick Facts: Sylvia Rhone

  • Before her career in music, she got a job at Bankers Trust in New York City.
  • She was a northeast regional promotion manager for the Elektra family in 1980.
  • Despite being born in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, she grew up in Harlem.
  • She completed her graduation with a B.S in Economics at the University of Pennsylvania.
  • She is also a member of the Board of Directions of Alvin Ailey American Dance Theater.
