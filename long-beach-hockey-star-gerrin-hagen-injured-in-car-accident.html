Long Beach Hockey Star Gerrin Hagen Injured in Car Accident: What Happened?

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Wednesday, July 3, 2024

Long Beach hockey defenseman Gerrin Hagen met a tragic crash this week that injured him severely. Read on to know everything about the car accident of hockey player Gerrin Hagen, what happened to him, and how is his condition right now.

The Isles community is currently praying for Gerrin Hagen who is struggling for his life in a hospital. He needs prayers more than anything for a miraculous recovery. Requests for prayers are surfacing online for the severely injured hockey star.

Who is Gerrin Hagen?

Gerrin Hagen is an 18-year-old ice hockey player from Long Beach, NY. He is a defenseman for his team and one of the most promising budding talents in the state. He played for the Long Island Gulls in Under-18 AAA.

Hagen was born on December 26, 2004. His height is 178 CM and weighs 78 KG. He had also played for Long Island Lightning, Long Beach High, and Long Island Edge. The defenseman always amazed the audience when he is on the field.

Gerrin Hagen Car Accident: How did it happen?

Long Beach hockey star Gerrin Hagen met a tragic accident while he was sitting inside his parked car at school. Another car came skating toward his parked car and a scary collision took place. The on goers were astonished by the impact and the sound of the crash.

First responders were summoned to the site and Hagen was carried to the nearby hospital. He is currently admitted at the medical facility where the doctors are trying their best to save their lives. Hagen was injured critically in the crash with multiple wounds.

How is Gerrin Hagen right now?

Gerrin Hagen is still critical at the hospital. An update was shared online by his close friend who revealed that he is not yet out of danger and the doctors are putting their best efforts to stabilize his condition.
