TNT Title Match Confirmed For 12/6 AEW Dynamite, Updated Card

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, July 30, 2024

Former best friends and tag team partners will clash in a buzzworthy match on the December 6 episode of AEW Dynamite.

On the November 25 episode of AEW Collision, Adam Copeland sent a heated message to TNT Champion Christian Cage and challenged him to a title match on the December 6 episode of AEW Dynamite.

Cage responded to the challenge on the November 29 episode of Dynamite. He delivered an emotional message about their friendship, and when he tried to blindside Copeland, “The Rated-R Superstar” hit him below the belt. Copeland then told Cage that he would be taking the title home with him next week. AEW subsequently confirmed the title match for the December 6 episode of AEW Dynamite.

Plus, AEW World Champion MJF agreed to face The Devil’s attackers in a tag team match after Samoa Joe saved him from the mysterious individuals on Wednesday. Additionally, the show will likely have AEW Continental Classic matches, but they have not been confirmed as of this writing.

12/6 AEW Dynamite Card

The updated card for the December 6 episode of Dynamite is as follows:

  • : Christian Cage (c) vs. Adam Copeland
  • MJF & Samoa Joe vs. TBA
