Ashley Monroe is an American singer and songwriter known for her singles like I Don’t Want To and Satisfied. She is a member of the country music band Pistol Annies and has also collaborated with popular country music artists. Ashley has a self-titled YouTube channel with more than 20k subscribers. She has a huge social media fan base with more than 200k followers on Instagram and more than 200k followers on Twitter.

    Ashley Monroe Facts

  • At the age of 15, she moved to Nashville with her mother to focus more on her country music career.
  • She decided to write the song Hard on a Heart after her therapist noted her abnormal heart rate and suggested that she should write a letter to her heart.
  • At the age of 11, she participated in a country music contest held at the iconic country singer Dolly Parton’s hometown of Pigeon Forge, Tennessee. She performed I Want to Be a Cowboy’s Sweetheart and went on to win the competition.
  • When she was 13 years old, her father bought her a guitar. She roped in the help of her cousin who lived up the street to teach her the cords.
  • Her debut album was set to be released in 2006 but it got shelved off as Columbia Records wanted her singles to do better before they released her album.
  • After a long dispute, Monroe decided to leave Columbia Records as she sought to build her career in her own way.
  • In July 2021, she revealed that she was diagnosed with rare blood cancer – Waldenström macroglobulinemia (WM). It is a type of non-Hodgkin’s lymphoma (aka lymphoplasmacytic lymphoma).
