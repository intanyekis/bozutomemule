Rich Wakile

Birthname Richard Wakile Education Businessman and TV Personality Profession Businessman Net worth $5,000,000 Source of Wealth Business, Television Nationality United States Marital Status Married to Kathy wakile Children Victoria and Joseph

Rich Wakile is a Lebanese-American businessman and reality star with an estimated net worth of $5 million. He is best known as the husband of 21 years to Kathy Wakile, one of the stars of the reality show The Real Housewives of New Jersey on Bravo TV. He also has appeared on the show along with his wife which made him a household name.

Rich and Kathy now live in Wayne, New Jersey together with their two kids, Victoria and Joe. Kathy has been a member of RHONJ for two seasons. She is very passionate with food and is currently working on her own line of deserts called Dolci della Dea or Goddess of Sweats. She also has a line of jewelry called Goddess Eye. The couple is a big supporter of the National Brain Tumor Society. Their daughter Victoria has a benign brain tumor. In order to help other people with similar case, they donate of portion of their business proceeds to the institution.
