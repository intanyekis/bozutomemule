Ariel Tweto is a Millionaire by 31; Let's Find Out How?

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Friday, August 9, 2024

“In life, you need three things to be happy; something to do, something to love, and something to hope for.” The Alaskan star Ariel Tweto is in love with this quote!

Tweto, who came to fame from her appearance in Wipeout and Flying Wild Alaska, has already had six acting credits under her name. From such, she must’ve made massive earnings, right? Without further ado, let’s dive into the lady’s professional career and see how much she amassed from such.

Millionaire by 31: Ariel Tweto

The 31 years’ old Ariel Tweto is already a millionaire as she has amassed as much as $1.2 million as her total net worth as of 2019. She made her worth from her career in the showbiz and from flying planes.

The lady first came to fame from her appearance in Total Wipeout. She’s known to the series’ fans as the Pole Blooper Girl. Why? Well, she was just yelling where the pole was though that pole was just in front of her.

YouTube: Ariel Tweto Looks For the Pole?

Following this, the Discovery Channel reached out to her family and created the show, Flying Wild Alaska. The show ran for a whole year from 2011 to 2012 with 31 episodes. Besides, Flying Wild Alaska, the lady also guest-starred in The Late Late Show with Craig Ferguson a number of times. She’s been on the show for around 16 episodes.

Ariel Tweto as a Pilot

The Native Shorts host Ariel Tweto is a licensed pilot. Her flying training was also captured on her show, Flying Wild Alaska.

After training for a couple of months, Ariel Tweto received her pilot’s license in April 2012. Now, she is the official management head of her family business, Era Alaska. She took up the job after her father’s retirement.

Ariel Tweto against Suicide

The 31 years’ old Ariel Tweto is standing up against suicide. In comparison to the other States of America, Alaska sees more percentage of death per year. Hence, Tweto has established an organization to end it.

The organization is known as, PoppingBubbles. From her organization, she tries to create programs that help foster the needs of individuals.

Some people might be curious about why the name, PoppingBubbles. For such, this idea came when Ariel had an interview with the CBC Radio, where she revealed,

We all live in little bubbles (never leaving the village, hanging out with only certain people, not trying a new food, not being open to different ethnicities, religions, ways of life, etc.)  …so I want to go around and pop them!

If you want any assistance, they provide, motivational public speaking tours, education media, community enrichment programs, organized tournaments, and integrative online social network.

Ariel Tweto Hasn’t Kissed Anyone in Her Life

In one of her interviews, the pilot, Ariel Tweto, revealed that she hasn’t, ever in her life, kissed anyone. Following this, she received a nickname, ‘Kissaholic.’ Similarly, since the show had talked about her not kissing anyone, the episode was referred to as, ‘The Kissing Episode.’

This very thing about not kissing anyone reveals that she is single! Once there were rumors of her dating her flying instructor but, it was not the truth. Ariel cleared the rumors stating the fact that she regards him as her brother and that’s about it.

Instead of having a partner, she indulges herself with her siblings and her pet dog. Her name is Lucy.

Early Life of Ariel Tweto

Ariel Tweto was born as Ariel Eva Tuadraq-Atauchaq Tweto on December 14, 1987, at Unalakleet, Alaska, USA, to father Jim and mother Freno Tweto. Her father was a pro hockey player; however, later, he changed his career to aviation after meeting his partner, Freno.

Ariel was brought up with her two siblings, one of whom is Ayla Tweto. She usually posts pictures with her sisters and spends a ton of time with them.

Grew up in Nome Census, Ariel enrolled at the Emerson College. She graduated with a major in communication and minor in broadcast journalism from Chapman University in Southern California.

Quick Facts: Ariel Tweto

  • Ariel says she loves meeting and chatting with people, getting lost, and exploring the wilderness.
  • She’s an avid traveler.
  • Though she doesn’t look fierce, she was on her high school’s co-ed wrestling team.
  • She still remembers shooting her first Caribou (Reindeer) with her grandma at age 9.
  • Her favorite quote is, “Dance like nobody is watching, laugh like you don’t care, live life to the fullest.”
  • She wants to create an animated series, Ariel’s View, where she will befriend various animal species and go on adventures.
