Mike Alstott Biography

Mike Alstott is an American former football fullback who was born and brought up in Joliet, Illinois. He played the National Football League for 12 seasons with the Tampa Bay Buccaneers.

He also played college football at Purdue. In his career, Alstott was selected to six Pro Bowls and was the starting fullback/running back with the Buccaneers in Super Bowl XXXVII.
