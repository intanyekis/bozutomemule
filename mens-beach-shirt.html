The Top Mens Beach Shirt of 2023 Reviews by Hollywood Life Hollywood Life

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, June 13, 2024

After what seemed like a much longer and colder winter than usual, the temperature has finally begun to rise. Now is a wonderful time to take note of your wardrobe and ditch the bulky, cold-weather attire in pursuit of something more airy, breezy, and vibrantly colored instead.

The best place to begin is by stocking up on a few decent beach shirts. This season's fashion trends include short-sleeved tops, light materials, pastel colors, and eye-catching designs. Loose-fitting but not baggy shirts are ideal for spending a day seaside. Linen, cotton, poplin, and Tencel are great for keeping you cool in hot weather because of their breathability. For your convenience, we are here with all need-to-know information to purchase top men’s beach shirts in 2023.

What Are the Most Critical Features of Men’s Beach Shirts?

Versatility

A general perception is that the beach shirts are specifically made for beach activities only. However, the truth is quite different. You can wear these at home and to any type of casual event whether it’s a random hang out with your friends or a movie night with the love of your life.

You might have seen some Hollywood stars wearing such beach shirts during a casual walk outside with their dogs. In short, the versatile nature offers much more than just rocking a beach party. These are tailor-made options for all the summer activities. Just buy a pair of sunglasses and a pair of classic beach shorts to pair up with your airy shirt and you will be all ready to rock for the whole of the summer season.

Quick-drying

What do you think makes beach shirts the most-suitable men’s wear for the summers? Most beach shirts dry quickly, allowing you to wear them again after a short time. The fabric of most shirts is lightweight in nature, and is also porous, allowing the water to evaporate more quickly.

Even if you are stuck in the rain or spend the whole day in the water, the finest beach shirts will not feel heavy on your upper body. Summers are usually associated with body sweat and extra moisture and wearing these sweat-wicking shirts will solve such issues instantly.

Material

The lightweight material is one of the most important considerations. It’s a no-brainer that you should avoid purchasing wool, cashmere, and other thick, insulating knits. Think terrycloth, cotton, and linen for an amazing overall experience. If you're looking to stay cool in the blazing heat, natural fibers are your best choice.

Quality

The men’s beach shirt may look lightweight in nature, but that does not make them a cheap or inferior-quality option by any means. In fact, the material used for these shirts is always up to the mark. The floral printing also adjusts well with the fabric type and the color will stay intact for longer durations. Cotton is generally the most common fabric type for summer wear like beach shirts since it’s easy to wash and remain comfy for longer durations.

Sleeve length

You should always opt for shirts with shorter sleeves for beach shirts. Your forearms are the actual star of the show, as well as the new watch that has been resting under your sleeve for too long. We think it's high time to show off that piece.

What Should You Wear With a Beach Shirt?

Denim shorts

Beach shirts are so classy, stylish, and modish that they fit well with most apparel types. The best combination is to wear these shirts with denim shorts of knee-high lengths. It is perhaps the most trendy option for beach activities in the summer months.

Sunglasses

Once you are done with buying the perfect denim shorts, now is the time to look for a classic and elegant pair of sunglasses. Beach shirts and black sunglasses are a match made in heaven as they both complement each other.

Sneakers or sandals

Coming to footwear, you can always go with sneakers of any type whenever you plan to go out with your friends. A pair of lightweight and open sandals can also be a good idea for a classy and stylish look. So, waste no more time and get yourself a colorful beach shirt with floral prints and you are ready to rock all the upcoming summer beach parties.

Why Do Beach Shirts Come in Loose-Fit Styles?

It is perhaps the most distinguishing characteristic of beach shirts that they come in a loose-fit style. They usually look a size larger than your body size but still give a classier look to your personality. It is because these are meant mainly for the summers and beach parties where men want to be as relaxed and comfortable as possible. In such situations, tight clothes will not be comfortable at all and the beach shirts with a loose-fit style should be your go-to option. Moreover, loose-fit beach shirts keep you cool even on the hottest days.
