Early Life & Biography

He was born on 1st March in 1953 in New York. He is the son of Gloria Frangione and Charles Zito Sr. He is the son of a welterweight boxer who was quite famous. Due to his father’s profession, he got influenced and learned to defend and fight at a young age. When he was just seventeen, he dropped out of high school to marry Kathy.

Personal Life

He got married to his girlfriend of high school, Kathy. They lived happily until he got involved with the HELL ANGELS motorcycle club. They haven’t revealed much about their divorce reasons, but it must be because of their conflicts.

Age, Height, and Weight

Career

He is a motorcycle lover and established NEW ROCHELLE MOTORCYCLE CLUB, and it merged with CHING-A-LING NOMADS later. He left Nomads and then joined HELLS ANGELS, and in 1984, he became the founder of HELLS ANGELS NEW YORK NOMAD CHAPTER. In the year 2005, he left HELLS ANGELS and focused only on his acting career.

He followed his father steps when it came to boxing and fought in NEW YORK GOLDEN GLOVES, and he used to work as a manual laborer. In the year 1980, he became the bodyguard of Robert Conrad. He decided to start his own bodyguard company and named it CHARLIE’S ANGELS BODYGUARD SERVICES. He has also been the bodyguard of Lorna Luft. The actress also recommended him to several celebrities. This led Zito to develop contacts faster in Hollywood.

In the movie, YEAR OF THE DRAGON, he started his career as a stuntman. He also worked in films like THE ROCK and HUDSON HAWK. In 1996, he met Tom Fontana (producer) and then joined OZ (prison drama of HBO), where he worked as the character of CHUCKY PANCAMO.

He has worked as a stuntman and actor in movies like THIS THING OF OURS and FIFTEEN MINUTES. On 11th January, in 1999, he appeared in WCW MONDAY NITRO with Hulk Hogan. He is also seen with Dwayne Johnson and Cuba Gooding at Mike Tyson Vs. Lennox Lewis fight in Memphis Pyramid. In the year 2003, his autobiography titled STREET JUSTICE was released, and JOE LAYDEN co-authored it.

In 2006, he started a radio show, CHUCK ZITO’s VIEW, which was liked by fans in the United States. In the year 2010, he filed a lawsuit of $5 million against the FX channel. He alleged that they had a developing meeting in 2006 in which he gave the idea of a motorcycle group. He told that FX channel stole his idea for the show, SONS OF ANARCHY.

On 1st December, in the year 2011, court judgment got ruled against him, and he appeared in the fifth SONS OF ANARCHY. He has also appeared in OUTLAW EMPIRES (documentary series by Discovery Channel). In the year 2013, he competed on the CHOPPED TV channel with several other stars. He is an all-rounder actor who struggled a lot in his initial days, but eventually, he made his career shining. He is a great person who is an inspiration for everyone.
