'The Masked Singer' Season 10 Elton John Night Spoiler: Billie Jean King is Royal Hen, here are the

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, June 3, 2024

LOS ANGELES, CALIFORNIA: Royal Hen got unmasked in 'The Masked Singer' Season 10 Elton John Night. The masked singer was then revealed to be 39-time Grand Slam Champion and activist Billie Jean King.

Well, it seems fans were certain about the celebrity singer's identity ahead of the episode's premiere. Seeing the small stature of the celebrity under the Royal Hen mask, fans placed their bet on the American former numero uno tennis player.

Since, 'The Masked Singer' Season 10 Episode 5 is a tribute to singer Elton John, and Billie inspired the British singer to write the song 'Philadelphia Freedom', fans were certain that it is the legendary player who is beneath the mask.

After seeing the Royal Hen's performance and hearing the voice of a woman, fans claimed that it was that of an old woman. Fans speculated that since Billie's name has 'King' in it, the Royal Hen's name is also a clue to Billie's identity.

'The Masked Singer' Season 10 'Elton John Night': Clues about Royal Hen

Royal Hen is claimed to be the recycled French Hen costume from 'The Masked Singer' Season 9. The costume appears to be a real hen and the uniform resembls that of a naval officer in an earlier era.

EW has also revealed a few clues for this masked singer. The Royal Hen's clue package mentions that the unknown celebrity 'holds court' and is a 'true trailblazer'.

The celebrity singer is claimed to be 'championing equal opportunities for all and has made it her royal mission to be a voice for the voiceless'.

The clue package also makes several sports references, including that this personality has 'inspired a new flock of birds' and the masked singer's 'name is etched in history'.

What will 'The Masked Singer' Season 10 Episode 5 be about?

'The Masked Singer' Season 10 Episode 5 will be dedicated to singer Elton John. Celebrating his music, the new masked singers will sing his songs specifically.

Episode 5 will feature four new masked singers in it. These new entries are - Royal Hen, Tiki, Husky, and Hawk.

'The Masked Singer' Season 10 releases new episodes every Wednesday at 8 pm ET on FOX.
