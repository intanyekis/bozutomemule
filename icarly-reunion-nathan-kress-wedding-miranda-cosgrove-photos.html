Cast Comes Together For Nathan Kress Wedding Photos Hollywood Life

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, June 18, 2024

Honestly, this is almost too precious to talk about. The show went off in 2012, but the cast members are still besties. Miranda, Jennette and Jerry all met up before the wedding just so they could catch a ride together. Aw! Miranda posted the adorable photo with the caption, “Heading to @nathankress’s wedding. Being old is so fun.” Hey! You guys aren’t that old!

As the wedding festivities continued, Miranda made sure to keep us updated on the iCarly reunion. The cast posed for silly pictures in a photo booth. So cute!

If you don’t think this iCarly reunion is enough, Miranda has given us all hope for a TV reunion in the future. At the NBC Upfronts in May 2015, Miranda teased a reunion and what she would like to see for her character!

“That would be fun,” Miranda told MTV News. “I mean, I love the cast of the show. I see them all the time. Jeanette is like, one of my best friends. And I see Jerry all the time, so I think that would be really fun…At the end of the show she moves to Italy, so I’d like her to come back to Seattle and get back to the way things used to be.”

HollywoodLifers, do you want an iCarly TV reunion to happen? Let us know your thoughts below!
