Recent Interview Revelations: Tems Hints at a Relationship

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Saturday, May 18, 2024

An interview with Beats FM provided insight into Nigerian singer Tems’ romantic status. When asked about collaborating with fellow artist Asake, Tems expressed praise for Asake’s artistry and interest in working together.

Despite this, when questioned about any romantic involvement with Asake, Tems dismissed the idea with laughter. Playfully suggesting she was already in a relationship, she told the interviewer, “Me? You can have him. I have my own.” Tems chose to keep the details of her relationship private.

Earlier, Tems had reflected on her Grammy Award win, casually mentioning that she likely keeps it on a shelf somewhere. She won the Grammy for her viral song “Wait for You,” featuring a snippet of one of her previous songs.

In 2022, Tems candidly shared her past financial struggles, recalling times of hunger and relying on her aunt for meals. She expressed disbelief at her current success given her previous financial hardship.

Tems also discussed her self-esteem issues, admitting to feeling unattractive and doubting her singing talent.
