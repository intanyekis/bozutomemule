Randy Orton Competes In Dark Match Main Event After WWE Raw Tonight

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, September 3, 2024

After tonight’s WWE Raw went off the air, Randy Orton vs Bray Wyatt took place in the dark match main event of the evening.

Very similar to their live event match over the weekend, Orton got the win tonight when he reversed a Sister Abigail attempt into an RKO.
